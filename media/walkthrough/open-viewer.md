# Live Log Viewer

The sidebar viewer shows your debug output in real-time with powerful features.

## Features

- **ANSI Colors** - Full color support for styled output
- **Virtual Scrolling** - Handle 100K+ lines without lag
- **Collapsible Stacks** - Click stack traces to expand/collapse
- **Click-to-Source** - Click `file.ts:42` to jump to that line
- **Pattern Highlighting** - Errors red, warnings yellow automatically

## Controls

- Scroll normally to browse output
- Auto-scroll resumes when you scroll to bottom
- Click the pause indicator in footer to see current state
