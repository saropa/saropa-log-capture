# DB_14 — Hypotheses strip (manual QA)

This extension has **no** Dart `analysis_options.yaml`; use this file as a **paste-in log** to exercise the viewer.

## What to expect

1. **Hypotheses** panel appears **above** the log (not inside the scroller) when signals are strong enough.
2. Copy says **Hypothesis, not fact.** Evidence buttons scroll to **line N** (1-based label, 0-based index); invalid indices after trim are omitted.
3. **Dismiss (×)** hides the strip for the **current log** until you **Clear** the viewer or open another session (session epoch resets).

## Minimal error-only signal

Paste a few normal lines, then one line the viewer classifies as **error** (plain text containing e.g. `Error:` / `Exception` per level classifier). You should see at least one **Recent error** bullet if the excerpt is long enough (≥ 4 non-whitespace chars).

## N+1 + fingerprint (Drift SQL)

Use `drift-n-plus-one-sample-lines.txt` with **DB insights enabled** in settings. After the burst, you should see:
- Synthetic **N+1** insight rows in the log, and
- A **Possible N+1** hypothesis pointing at the synthetic line.

## Weak session (should NOT show strip)

- Only a couple of short `e=` / `ab` style messages with no error level and no DB rollup — **no** Hypotheses strip.
- Fingerprint hit count below the leader threshold (see `ROOT_CAUSE_FP_LEADER_MIN_COUNT` in `root-cause-hint-eligibility.ts`) without errors or N+1 — **no** strip.
